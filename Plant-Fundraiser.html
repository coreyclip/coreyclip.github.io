Plants for a cause

Affordable plants to brighten your home, 100% of proceeds go directly for the Cholangiocarcinoma foundation and Fran's place. In Loving memory of Grace Clippinger, who's passion for rescuing abandoned office plants inspired this effort.

Story: Since my mother's passing I've been taking care of and propogating the plants my mother has rescued from her old office. During my mom's illness and the aftermath of her passing the Cholangiocarcinoma Foundation which works specifically on the kind of cancer my mother had and Fran's place, a counseling non-profit that works with cancer patients and their families have been invaluable resources for me and my family. So to help give back I decided to try and raise money by transforming my new hobby into a fund raising opportunity for these two remarkable organizations. 100% of all purchases will go to both organizations and If requested I will include a receipt of the final donation amount via email.

Spider Plants

Easy to care for and grow, named for the vines that produce saplings of the parent plant that look like spiders.

Peace Lilies

Glossy green leaves and when mature produce beautiful spoon shaped white flowers. Do well by a windows inside.

Vegetables and herbs

Raised from seed chili pepper plants, mint, and cherry tomatoes these tend to do well in sunny parts of the home or outside.

Succulents

I also have a bunch of succulents that I've propagated. These are easy to care for, just a lot of sun and limit watering.
