When throwing Darts at a map

In the process of generating random lat/long values for this project I noted that fairly often the closest city to these randomly generated points returned duplicates. I adjusted my code so that I could capture and record these instances. The result is this graph Since there's an equal chance for any area on the globe to be picked by the random number generator. A fair number of our duplicates are in the southern hemisphere, where there is a much higher chance of picking a lat/long value in the middle of the ocean. Therefore our top 10 duplicates, for the most part, share these qualities
  • Isolated
  • Coastal
  • Located in the South Pacific
