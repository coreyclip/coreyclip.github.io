Outline of the Earth

By plotting temperature against our latitude values we get a image that basically outlines a tilted sphere. The data for this project was scraped back in Winter of 2018. It's notable that the graph reflects the fact that this shows that it's currently winter in the Northern Hemisphere. At the higher latitude values the rate of temperature drop increases as we move away from the equator. While in the Southern Hemisphere the we see more milder temperatures. This is due to the planet's axil tilt. At this point the earth is tilted southwards, leading to warmer and milder temperatures in the south.
