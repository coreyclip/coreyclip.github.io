Mountain Weather

One major factor effecting atmospheric pressure is altitude. In this graph we can see that while most area have pressure values close to 1000 which is equal to the average pressure at sea level. There is a scattering of cities at various latitude lines that have much lower atmospheric pressure indicating cities located at higher elevations.
