Dry skin to the North and South

At latitude values close to zero aka around the equator for the most part humidity is high, but as we move away from the equator towards the poles we see areas that are considerably drier. Note we also see that in general we mostly get humidity data from northern latitudes rather than southern ones this is likely due to the fact that there is less landmass in the south than in the north.
